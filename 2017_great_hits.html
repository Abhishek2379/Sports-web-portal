2017 Great Hits

It was just a question of belief' - Parthiv Patel

Parthiv Patel could not have scripted a better end to the 2016-17 Ranji Trophy. He first scored 90 to set up a lead and followed it with 143 to seal the highest successful chase in a final.

Patel: Basically, it's because of a good plan back in Gujarat. Before the start of the season, we play a lot of practice matches. When we sit to pick the team, our meeting doesn't last more than four-five minutes because the players pick themselves on their performances. We play so many matches before the start of the season that we are ready to hit the ground running. The system for junior cricket is very good and those who perform in district cricket get an opportunity to play in the KSCA or Buchi Babu tournament.




Knights crowned Sunfoil Series champs

The VKB Knights were crowned champions of the 2016-2017 Sunfoil Series after beating the bizhub Highveld Lions by an innings and 121 runs at the Bidvest Wanderers Stadium in Johannesburg on Saturday.

Nicky van der Bergh and Rassie van der Dussen were the overnight batsmen who were tasked with keeping the lethal quartet of Knights seamers in Duanne Olivier, Marchant de Lange, Shadley van Schalkwyk and Mbulelo Budaza at bay.




Peshawar Zalmi defeated Quetta Gladiators on PSL - 2

The final was held in Lahore on 5 March and saw Peshawar Zalmi defeating Quetta Gladiators by 58 runs to win the championship. Darren Sammy of Peshawar Zalmi was awarded the man of the match award. Kamran Akmal also of Peshawar Zalmi was named the player of the tournament.

The closing ceremony was held at Gaddafi Stadium in Lahore. It was hosted by Ayesha Omar and Ahmad Ali Butt. It featured live performances of Pakistani singers Ali Zafar, Faakhir Mehmood, Overload band and Ali Azmat. The crowd was sold out and spectators enjoyed the closing ceremony.




Mumbai Indians beat Rising Pune Supergiants to win their third title

The Mumbai Indians were on Sunday night crowned VIVO IPL 2017 Champions. In a match befitting of a tournament finale, MI won their third IPL title after they defeated Rising Pune Supergiant by a solitary run at the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium in Hyderabad.

Despite an underwhelming performance with the bat, and a sloppy performance in the field, the MI bowlers applied the squeeze on their opponents, eventually leading their team to a 1-run win. After scoring a below-par 129-8, MI restricted RPS to 128-6.
